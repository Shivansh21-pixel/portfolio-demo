The Best Movies According to Shivansh Pandey

My top 3 movies of all time

Vivah

"Vivah" is known for its portrayal of traditional Indian culture and customs, especially in the context of marriage. It combines a melodious soundtrack with a family-oriented narrative, reflecting the values and traditions of Indian society.

Oppenheimer

"Oppenheimer" (2023), directed by Christopher Nolan, is a biographical film about J. Robert Oppenheimer, the American physicist known as the "father of the atomic bomb." The film stars Cillian Murphy as Oppenheimer and delves into his complex life and career.

Maharaja

The movie's plot incorporates political intrigue, personal vendettas, and social issues, all while offering a mix of action-packed sequences and dramatic moments. It is notable for its strong performances, engaging storyline, and the way it addresses complex themes related to power and influence.
